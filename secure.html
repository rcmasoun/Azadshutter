Steel Doors

We provide durable, secure, and long-lasting Steel Doors for industrial, commercial, and residential applications. Our steel doors are designed to offer superior security, fire resistance, and weatherproofing, making them a perfect solution for environments that demand strength and safety.

Key Features:

  • High-Quality Steel: Built from premium-grade steel for enhanced durability and security.
  • Fire-Resistant Options: Available in fire-rated models to protect against fire hazards.
  • Weatherproof: Engineered to resist harsh weather conditions and prevent rust or corrosion.
  • Customizable: Available in various sizes, thicknesses, finishes, and designs to meet specific needs.
  • Secure: Reinforced locks, hinges, and frames for maximum security.

Types of Steel Doors:

Standard Steel Doors

Description:

Standard steel doors provide general-purpose security and durability. They are commonly used for both residential and commercial entryways.

Best For:

Homes, offices, shops, and storage units.

Features:

  • Made from galvanized steel for strength and rust resistance.
  • Available in a variety of finishes such as painted, powder-coated, or primed for further painting.
  • Can be customized with different locking mechanisms.

Fire-Rated Steel Doors

Description:

These doors are designed to prevent the spread of fire and smoke in case of an emergency, providing critical protection in high-risk environments.

Best For:

Factories, warehouses, hospitals, and public buildings where fire safety regulations are in place.

Features:

  • Fire-resistance ratings ranging from 30 minutes to 3 hours, depending on the model.
  • Tested and certified to meet fire safety standards.
  • Automatic closing mechanism in case of fire alarms or emergencies.
  • Available in single or double-door configurations.

Fire-Rated Glass Doors

Description:

Doors featuring fire-resistant glass to allow visibility while maintaining fire protection.

Best For:

Areas where transparency is needed, such as office partitions, showrooms, and lobbies.

Features:

  • Fire-rated glass that maintains integrity during a fire.
  • Available with various frame materials and finishes.
  • Often includes automatic closing mechanisms and smoke seals.

Stainless Steel Doors

Description:

These doors are made from stainless steel for superior corrosion resistance and aesthetics. Ideal for environments that demand durability, hygiene, and style.

Best For:

Hospitals, laboratories, kitchens, and coastal properties.

Features:

  • Rust-proof and highly resistant to corrosion, even in moist environments.
  • Hygienic and easy to clean, making them suitable for sterile environments.
  • Available in brushed, polished, or matte finishes.
  • Sleek, modern design with excellent durability.

Acoustic Steel Doors

Description:

Acoustic steel doors are specially designed to provide soundproofing. They are ideal for areas where noise control is critical.

Best For:

Recording studios, auditoriums, theatres, and meeting rooms.

Features:

  • Soundproofing properties, reducing noise transmission by up to 50dB or more.
  • Available with fire-rated or security options.
  • Sealed edges to prevent sound leakage.
  • Customizable finishes and designs.

Blast-Resistant Steel Doors

Description:

Blast-resistant steel doors are engineered to withstand high-impact forces, such as explosions, making them essential in high-risk facilities.

Best For:

Military facilities, oil and gas plants, chemical facilities, and power stations.

Features:

  • Constructed to endure extreme pressures and blasts.
  • High-security locking systems for added protection.
  • Can be customized to meet specific blast-resistance standards.
  • Available in single or double configurations with various locking mechanisms.

Bulletproof Steel Doors

Description:

Bulletproof steel doors provide ballistic protection, making them essential in areas where security from firearms is critical.

Best For:

Banks, government buildings, high-security facilities, and embassies.

Features:

  • Tested and rated to withstand different ballistic levels (NIJ ratings).
  • Laminated with bullet-resistant materials.
  • Available with armoured locks and hinges for added security.
  • Can be combined with fire-resistant and acoustic features.

Customized Steel Doors

Description:

We also offer fully customized steel doors tailored to the exact specifications and requirements of your project.

Best For:

Projects with unique requirements for size, design, or functionality.

Features:

  • Custom sizes, thicknesses, and finishes.
  • Wide variety of colours and textures.
  • Choice of hinges, locks, and hardware.
  • Available in single or double door configurations.

Benefits of Steel Doors

  • High Security: Steel doors provide superior protection against break-ins, vandalism, and unauthorized access.
  • Fire Resistance: Fire-rated models offer crucial protection in emergency situations, helping to contain fire and smoke.
  • Durability: Built to last, steel doors are resistant to wear, weather, and impact.
  • Low Maintenance: Requires minimal upkeep compared to wooden or fiberglass doors.
  • Customizable: Available in a wide range of designs, sizes, and finishes to suit various architectural styles.

Customization Options:

  • Materials: Galvanized steel, stainless steel, or reinforced steel.
  • Finishes: Painted, powder-coated, primed, or brushed stainless steel.
  • Locking Systems: Panic Bar, Standard locks, deadbolts, multi-point locks, biometric locks.
  • Fire Ratings: Available in 30, 60, 90, 120, and 180-minute fire ratings.
  • Size and Design: Customizable to your specific needs and preferences.

Applications:

  • Industrial: Factories, warehouses, plants, and high-risk areas.
  • Commercial: Offices, retail outlets, hospitals, and public buildings.
  • Residential: Homes, apartment complexes, and private properties.
  • High-Security: Government buildings, banks, data centres, and vaults.
