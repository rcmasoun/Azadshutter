Aluminium Work

We offer a range of high-quality aluminium products designed for durability and modern aesthetics. Our aluminium solutions include doors, windows, partitions, aluminium composite panel wall cladding, and glazing work.

Types of Rolling Shutters

Aluminium Doors

Aluminium doors are durable and offer a modern, sleek appearance. They are resistant to rust and corrosion, making them ideal for both residential and commercial use.

Key Features:

  • Durability: High resistance to weather conditions and wear.
  • Design: Available in various styles and finishes.
  • Security: Options for high-security locking systems.
  • Customization: Can be tailored to specific sizes and designs.

Types:

  • Sliding Aluminium Doors: Smooth sliding mechanism for space-saving.
  • Hinged Aluminium Doors: Traditional opening style for easy access.
  • Bi-Fold Aluminium Doors: Multi-panel doors that fold neatly for wide openings.

Aluminium Windows

Description:

Aluminium windows are known for their strength and low maintenance. They provide excellent insulation and can be fitted with double or triple glazing.

Features:

  • Energy Efficiency: Good thermal insulation properties.
  • Design: Available in a range of styles including casement, awning, and fixed windows.
  • Maintenance: Easy to clean and maintain.

Types:

  • Casement Windows: Hinged on one side and open outward.
  • Sliding Windows: Move horizontally to open and close.
  • Awning Windows: Hinged at the top and open outward.

ACP Facades

Description:

Aluminium Composite Panels (ACP) are used for exterior cladding. They offer a stylish and durable finish and are used in modern architecture for both aesthetics and protection.

Features:

  • Aesthetic Appeal: Available in various colours and finishes.
  • Durability: Resistant to weather, UV rays, and impact.
  • Ease of Maintenance: Simple cleaning and long-lasting.

Types:

  • Exterior ACP Panels: Designed for facades and building cladding.
  • Interior ACP Panels: Used for interior walls and partitions.

Glazing Work

Description:

Glazing work involves installing glass into windows, doors, and facades. It enhances the appearance and energy efficiency of a building.

Features:

  • Energy Efficiency: Provides insulation and reduces energy costs.
  • Aesthetic Value: Offers a modern and transparent look.
  • Custom Solutions: Can be tailored to specific requirements.

Types:

  • Double Glazing: Two layers of glass with a space between them for insulation.
  • Triple Glazing: Three layers of glass for enhanced insulation.
  • Tempered Glass: Heat-treated for extra strength and safety.

Benefits of Rolling Shutters

  • Longevity: High durability and low maintenance.
  • Customization: Wide range of designs and finishes available.
  • Environmental Impact: Aluminium is recyclable and eco-friendly.

Installation & Maintenance:

  • Professional Installation: Ensure proper fitting for optimal performance.
  • Routine Maintenance: Regular cleaning and checks to maintain appearance and functionality.

Applications:

  • Residential Doors and windows for homes.
  • Commercial: Facades, glazing, and high-traffic areas.
