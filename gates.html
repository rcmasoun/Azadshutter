Gates

AZAD ROLLING SHUTTERS AND CONSTRUCTION offers a wide range of Gates that blend security, elegance, and efficiency. Our gates are crafted to meet both residential and commercial needs, providing space-saving solutions while maintaining robust security.

Key Features:

  • High-Quality Materials Crafted using premium-grade steel or aluminium for maximum strength and durability.
  • Smooth Operation: Precision-engineered tracks and rollers ensure a quiet and smooth opening and closing action.
  • Customization: Gates are tailored to your specific size, design, and finish requirements.
  • Manual or Automated Options: Available in manual or motorized versions with remote-controlled operation for added convenience.
  • Weather Resistance: Designed to withstand extreme weather conditions, providing long-term reliability with minimal maintenance.

Types of Sliding Gates

Sliding Gates

Description:

Sliding gates are ideal for properties where space is limited. They operate horizontally along a track, making them suitable for narrow driveways or areas with restricted space.

Features:

  • Space-Saving Design: Requires less clearance than swing gates.
  • Smooth Operation: Can be manually operated or equipped with an automatic sliding system.
  • Durability: Built to withstand various weather conditions.

Types:

  • Single Sliding Gate: Consists of a single panel that slides to one side.
  • Bi-Parting Sliding Gate: Features two panels that slide open from the centre, providing a wider entrance.

Applications:

  • Residential: Ideal for driveways and private property entrances.
  • Commercial: Suitable for parking lots, industrial facilities, and secure entry points.

Swing Gates

Description:

Swing gates operate by swinging open and closed on hinges. They are a versatile option, offering both aesthetic appeal and functionality.

Features:

  • Classic Design: Traditional look that can complement various architectural styles.
  • Ease of Use: Simple mechanism, can be manually operated or automated.
  • Customization: Available in various materials and designs.

Types:

  • Single Swing Gate: A single gate panel that swings open in one direction.
  • Double Swing Gate: Two gate panels that open in the middle, suitable for wider entrances.

Applications:

  • Residential: Front yards, driveways, and private entrances.
  • Commercial: Service areas, access points, and secure entryways.

Telescopic Gates

Description:

Telescopic gates are a variant of sliding gates, featuring overlapping panels that retract into each other, reducing the space needed for the gate to open.

Features:

  • Compact Design: Ideal for very narrow spaces where traditional sliding gates might be impractical.
  • Efficient Operation: Panels slide into each other, providing a space-efficient solution.

Applications:

  • Commercial: Limited spaces like narrow driveways or tight entrances.
  • Residential: Small residential driveways or side gates.

Automatic Gates

Description:

Automatic gates are operated via a motorized system, allowing for remote or automated control.

Features:

  • Convenience: Remote control or automated systems for ease of access.
  • Enhanced Security: Integration with access control systems for better security.
  • Customizable: Can be adapted to various gate types (sliding, swing, etc.).

Applications:

  • Residential: Automated home entrances and driveways.
  • Commercial: High-traffic areas requiring controlled access.

Foldable Gates

Description:

Foldable gates, also known as accordion-style gates, fold into sections like an accordion, making them a flexible option for varying entrance sizes.

Features:

  • Space-Saving: Folds into compact sections when open.
  • Versatile: Can be used for a variety of applications and entrance sizes.

Applications:

  • Commercial: Retail store entrances, warehouses, and service areas.
  • Residential: Gates for side entrances or smaller driveways.

  • Steel: Strong and durable, suitable for high-security needs.
  • Wood: Provides a classic look; requires regular maintenance.

Installation & Maintenance

Installation:

  • Professional Installation Ensures proper alignment and secure fitting for optimal functionality.
  • Track and Mechanism: For sliding gates, ensure the track is properly installed and maintained.

Maintenance:

  • Regular Checks: Inspect hinges, tracks, and mechanisms for wear and tear.
  • Cleaning: Regular cleaning to maintain appearance and functionality.
