HI-SPEED DOOR

The Hi-Speed Rapid Door does not only move with an incredible speed but also requires extremely low maintenance, is heavy-duty and extremely stable. The flexible door leaf is led laterally and is moved upwards and downwards in a force-closed manner: Linear expansions are ruled out. It consists of individually replaceable, 4-field module segments. Whether glowing yellow, warning red, bright blue or neutral grey. You can choose between several options with regard to the design of the curtain of your door leaf. There are additional colours possible upon request.
