Automated Entrance Solutions

We offer a wide range of automated entrance solutions designed to enhance security, streamline access control, and add convenience to commercial, industrial, and residential facilities. Our advanced products are built for durability, efficiency, and safety, making them ideal for high-traffic areas and sensitive entry points.

Key Products:

Boom Barriers:

Description:

Our automated boom barriers provide efficient control at entry and exit points. Widely used in parking lots, toll booths, gated communities, and commercial facilities, they ensure controlled vehicle access.

Applications:

Parking lots, gated communities, toll plazas, and industrial sites.

Features:

  • Fast opening and closing speeds.
  • Integration with access control systems (RFID, biometric, etc.).
  • Available in different lengths for various entry sizes.
  • Weatherproof and corrosion-resistant construction.

Spike Barriers:

Description:

Spike barriers allow vehicles to pass in one direction while preventing reverse entry with retractable spikes. They offer superior security in sensitive areas.

Applications:

Military bases, airports, high-security zones, and industrial complexes.

Features:

  • Heavy-duty steel spikes.
  • Integrated traffic light signal system.
  • Ideal for high-security areas.
  • Operates in sync with boom barriers for complete access control.

Bollards:

Description:

Our bollards are retractable posts designed for vehicle access control while allowing pedestrian movement. These high-impact-resistant bollards provide both security and aesthetic appeal.

Applications:

Commercial buildings, government offices, pedestrian zones, and urban infrastructure.

Features:

  • Automatic or semi-automatic operation.
  • High resistance to impact forces.
  • Stainless steel or powder-coated finishes.
  • Can be synchronized with access control systems.

Tripod Turnstiles:

Description:

Designed with an open grille pattern, these shutters provide security while allowing visibility and airflow. They are often used in shopping centres and commercial properties.

Applications:

Offices, metro stations, stadiums, amusement parks, and public buildings.

Features:

  • Compact, durable design.
  • Integration with RFID, fingerprint, or card readers.
  • Anti-tailgating technology.
  • Allows bi-directional movement.

High-Speed Roll-Up Doors:

Description:

High-speed roll-up doors are designed for environments where quick access and high performance are required. They offer fast operation, energy efficiency, and durability, making them ideal for industrial and commercial applications.

Applications:

Warehouses, distribution centres, clean rooms, and automotive facilities.

Features:

  • Rapid opening and closing speeds for high-traffic areas.
  • Energy-saving features to reduce heat loss.
  • Durable, weather-resistant construction.
  • Customizable with various safety features (sensors, breakaway systems, etc.).

Overhead Sectional Doors:

Description:

Overhead sectional doors provide robust and flexible solutions for both commercial and industrial use. These doors are divided into sections that glide smoothly on overhead tracks, offering efficient space utilization and enhanced security.

Applications:

Industrial warehouses, loading docks, workshops, and commercial garages.

Features:

  • Strong insulation to regulate indoor temperature.
  • Smooth and silent operation.
  • Available with windows for added visibility.
  • Can be automated for ease of use.
