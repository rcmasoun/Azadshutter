About Us

Founded in the early 1960s in Kota (Rajasthan)

Established in 1960, our company has over six decades of expertise in the manufacturer and supplier of high-quality rolling shutters, steel doors, gates, aluminium products, false ceilings, automated entrance products, and other fabrication works. We have built a strong reputation for delivering durable and reliable solutions that meet the diverse needs of our clients across various industries.

Why Choose Us?

With more than 55 years of experience of working in this space Azad Rolling Shutters & Constructions has provided services to many PSU companies and have undertaken large to very large shutter installation assignments.

Azad Rolling Shutters & Constructions is an approved vender to large number of public enterprises like BARC, NPCIL, NTPC, BRIDGE & ROOF, MAZGAON DOCK LIMITED, RCF, HAL, ISRO to name a few.

Apart from Rolling Shutters we are also engaged in manufacture of all types of entrance gates and doors for residential and commercial buildings there by making us complete entrance specialist.
