False Ceiling Solutions

We offer expert false ceiling solutions for residential, commercial, and industrial spaces. Our false ceilings are designed to enhance the aesthetic appeal of interiors while also providing functional benefits like soundproofing, insulation, and improved lighting.

Types of False Ceilings:

Gypsum False Ceiling:

  • Made from gypsum boards, which are lightweight and durable.
  • Known for their smooth finish and ability to be shaped into creative designs.
  • Suitable for both modern and traditional designs.
  • Fire-resistant and soundproof.

POP (Plaster of Paris) False Ceiling:

  • Made using plaster of Paris, a lightweight material that can be moulded into intricate shapes.
  • Offers flexibility in design, allowing for curved, detailed patterns.
  • Economical and widely used for decorative ceilings.

PVC False Ceiling:

  • Composed of lightweight PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) panels, ideal for moist areas like bathrooms and kitchens.
  • Easy to install and maintain.
  • Available in a variety of colours and designs.
  • Water-resistant and termite-proof.

Metal False Ceiling:

  • Constructed using aluminium or steel, providing a modern, industrial look.
  • Highly durable and fire-resistant.
  • Often used in commercial spaces like offices, showrooms, and malls.

Wooden False Ceiling:

  • Offers a warm and luxurious appearance.
  • Commonly used in living rooms, offices, and hotels.
  • Can be made from hardwood or engineered wood panels.
  • Requires regular maintenance to prevent warping or termite damage.

Glass False Ceiling:

  • Made from glass panels, often frosted or tinted.
  • Adds elegance and allows natural light to filter through.
  • Mostly used in high-end spaces, offices, and restaurants.

Benefits of False Ceilings:

Specifications:
  • Thickness: Varies based on material, typically 12-15 mm for gypsum, 20-25 mm for POP.
  • Dimensions: Standard panel sizes or customized as per project requirements.
  • Fire Resistance: Available with fire-resistant materials upon request.
  • Finish: Smooth or textured, with the option of painting or veneering.

Additional Services:

  • Lighting installation, including LED fixtures.
  • Integrated HVAC vents and air diffusers.
  • Customization based on specific design requirements.
