About Us

Founded in the early 1960s in Kota (Rajasthan)

Since 1960, AZAD ROLLING SHUTTERS AND CONSTRUCTION has been a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality rolling shutters, steel doors, gates, aluminium products, false ceilings, automated entrance products, and other fabrication works. Located in Kota, Rajasthan, we are committed to delivering excellence and innovation in every product we create.

  • Our legacy of over six decades speaks to our dedication to quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction.
  • We pride ourselves on offering bespoke solutions that meet the unique needs of our clients while adhering to the highest industry standards.
